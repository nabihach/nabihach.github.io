I am an NLP Engineer at Textio Inc. I recently completed my PhD in Computer Science from the University of Waterloo, Canada. My supervisor was Pascal Poupart, and I was a member of the Artificial Intelligence lab. I do engineering and research work at the intersection of Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing. Most of my PhD research was done in collaboration with Huawei and ProNavigator. I have also been affiliated with Vector Institute for Artificial Intelligence and Waterloo Artificial Intelligence Institute.

I received a Masters of Mathematics in Combinatorics & Optimization from the University of Waterloo. My masters research was in Graph Theory, under the supervision of R. Bruce Richter. I received a Bachelors of Science (Honours) in Mathematics and Computer Science from Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS). You can view my CV here (last updated: May 2019).
